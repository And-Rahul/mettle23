PROF RAMAMOORTHY RAMESH

SKYRMIONS: A NEW ERA IN FERROELECTRICS

With over 65,000 citations, our distinguished guest is a pioneer in the field of manganites and electric field control of ferromagnetism. He's an elected member of the US National Academy of Engineering and the Royal Society of London. He's a recipient of the TMS Bardeen Prize, the Alexander von Humboldt Senior Scientist Prize among several other laurels.
